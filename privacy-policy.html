Privacy Policy for OverTheload

Effective Date: 25.12.2024

OverTheload respects your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data or user information.

How We Use Information

- No Data Collection: OverTheload operates entirely on the user's device and processes data locally within the browser.
- Server Communication: The extension interacts with a backend service hosted at https://music-miner-ivelin3.replit.app/ to analyze extracted text. However, no personal user data is transmitted, stored, or shared.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To allow interaction with the currently active Reddit page for extracting text.
  • Host permissions: To enable communication with Reddit and the backend service.

Questions

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at ivelin@dialogdynamo.com .
